MicroEggs

Description

Simple and easy to make egg sandwich right from the microwave in 2 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • 2 slices of bread (Wonder Bread for optimal results)
  • Cheese slice (American ONLY! - 2 if you are not a rookie)
  • Salt
  • Rapid Egg Cooker (Dollar store gem)
  • Small container with lid (Just big enough to contain final sandwich)
  • Microwave

Steps

  1. Whip out your Rapid Egg cooker and crack your eggs into it
  2. Scrampble up dem eggs with a fork or whatever you have available
  3. Add a pinch of salt (Dont forget were using salty cheese, so dont go crazy. Especially if you not a rookie)
  4. Nuke it for 1 minute
  5. Stir the egs one good time, add 1 slice of cheese on top.
  6. Nuke for another 30 seconds. Eggs should still be a bit loose.
  7. Prep your containter with one slice of bread and one slice of cheese, if you aren't a rookie
  8. Slide the eggs from the Rapid Egg cooker cheese side up laying on top of the bread and cheese in the container.
  9. Place other bread slice on top and close the lid to your container for 30 secs or more.
  10. Pop that puppy out and devour!
